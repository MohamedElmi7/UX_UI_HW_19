UX Research

I approach user research much like a scientist would, following the scientific method. Always questioning and iterating to the end result.

USER EMPATHY

My approach for User Empathy is that I remind myself that I am not the user,so if I have any hypothesis on a subject I must test it through users and see how they feel about it. I do this through interviews and surveys; compiling frustrations, desires, and hopes. I do this through many applications such as affinity and empathy maps, an I Like-I Wish-What if matrix, and so much more.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

My approach to Qualitative Interviews is that I create an environment that is comfortable and engaging for  my interviewer subjects. As well as making sure that subjects match the my ideal goals such as matching a proto persona, for example. In hopes of  cultivating deeper interactions and breakthroughs. So being tactful with depth is who I am.
